Olympic Athlete Interactive Gender Static Tool

About this project

The Olympic Games are leading international sporting events being held in summer and winter, and thousands of athletes all over the world participate in a variety of competitions. Both male and female athletes bring us exciting games for audience.

In our project, our team members are going to have an in-depth study for both male and femal athletes from 1980 to 2016 Oylmpic Games. We will compare them from three different aspects, Personal Information (Height, Weight, Age), Medal Numbers (Gold, Silver, Bronze), and Top 10 Most Popular Sports.

Our project draws on data from 120 years of Olympic history: athletes and results from Kaggle user rgriffin, which includes infromation on athletes from 1896 to 2016, and we filter the dataset for those after 1980 for more recent, modern Olympic Games.
